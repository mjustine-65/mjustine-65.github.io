About Me

Thank you for visiting my web portfolio!

Hi! I'm Justine, from Metro Manila, and I have my associate's degree in Information Technology from Gateways Institute of Science and Technology. I'm a motorcyclist, but still new in the motorcycle world, who loves to go to the countryside to enjoy a beautiful view of the mountains, the sea, and the sunset. I love traveling on my bike as it gives me the freedom to be myself. Not only that, but it is also a way for me to get rid of stress. When I'm happy, I ride. When I'm sad, I ride. Whatever I'm feeling, I always ride because Dan Aykroyd once said, "You do not need a therapist if you own a motorcycle, any kind of motorcycle!". And the more I ride, the more I know about myself.

I used to work in the BPO industry as a customer service and technical support representative. In my most recent job, I supported our customers through email, phone calls, and chats using Zendesk. I like working with them because of how they give importance to teamwork. They encourage everyone to speak up, ask questions, provide suggestions, and share ideas when possible as it may help the team to improve the service to deliver to our clients. The job alone also pays well.

While working in the BPO industry is fulfilling, I'm pursuing my dream career in the tech industry as a full-stack web developer. I'm looking forward to securing a job with a company that will allow me to showcase and enhance the skills and knowledge I gained in doing a coding bootcamp with Zuitt along with my years of experience in the BPO industry. Through this web portfolio, I like to showcase to you what I can do so far with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
